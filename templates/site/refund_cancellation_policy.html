  • Once an order has been dispatched, cancellation may not be available and the return process will apply instead where eligible.
  • {{ site_business_name }} may cancel orders affected by payment failure, pricing errors, stock unavailability, or suspected misuse.

Return Eligibility

  • Return requests must generally be raised within {{ site_return_window_days }} days of delivery unless a product page or category states otherwise.
